Education

We all think that education in America sucks. We all have been bored in class and we have all been frustrated with how stupid some assignment was, no matter how much we actually do love learning. Because we all actually love learning. It's a very inherent human trait, yet the current system where one is to learn does not seem to tap into the natural inclination. Instead, school relies on extrinsitc rewards and punishments like grades. And school restricts what you can learn, when you can learn, and who you can learn with. It is my goal to transform the current behaviorist school system into a constructivist system. The constructivist viewpoint is that learning happens at an interaction between information from the envirnment and ideas from the learner. With this, I believe that students learn better when they are able to make those interactions by choice. I am heavily inspired by the work of Mitch Resnick.
Right now, I am working to create a platform where students are able to code, compose music, and draw all in one, with their mentor/teacher being an ai agent. To learn more about what I'm working on please email me!
